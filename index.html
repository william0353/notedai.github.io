About the App

NotedAI is a SwiftUI-based app that helps you study PDFs with structure and intelligence. Inspired by the Cornell Note-Taking Method, it allows you to take well-organized notes beside your documents and summarize each page with clarity.

In the note management page, you can sort, search, bookmark, and review all your notes with ease. For subscribers, the AI features provide article summaries, background analysis, author insights, and term extraction. You can even chat with a powerful AI anytime to enhance your understanding.
